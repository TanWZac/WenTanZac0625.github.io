Global Vaccination Rate

Vaccination is the only way to defeat Covid-19. The contrast is clear as most highest vaccination country are generally well-developed country and low vaccination rate country are mostly developing and less developed country.

Some countries has vaccination rate higher than 100, for example when over 50% of the population receives both required vaccine doses under the intended 2 dose regime

Regional Vaccination Rate

The bar chart shows each region vaccination status. The Euro region has the highest vaccination rate among the other region, followed by Western Pacific Region and Region of Americas.

Fully vaccinated and half vaccinated are accumlated using the cumulated number of persons per 100 populations, providing a more accurate insights.
